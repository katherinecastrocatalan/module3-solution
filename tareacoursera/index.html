El Pollo Loco is a Mexican roast chicken restaurant franchise with more than four hundred locations in the United States and Mexico. El Pollo Loco was founded on Monday, January 6, 1975 by Juan Francisco “Pancho” Ochoa in Guasave, Sinaloa, Mexico, it is one of the first Mexican restaurant franchises that successfully crossed the borders of Mexico. In 1974 Juan Francisco “Pancho” Ochoa decided to commercially exploit a family recipe that had been preserved through several generations. The recipe was a mix of seasonings, herbs, spices, and fruits added to the chicken roasting process. In the beginning Francisco Ochoa sold his chickens on a cart. On Monday, January 6, 1975, the First Pollo Loco was inaugurated in the then small town of Guasave, Sinaloa, which continues to operate to this day and over the years, the franchise has expanded to several cities in Mexico. The first restaurant in the United States opened on December 8, 1980 in Los Angeles, California. The franchises. The El Pollo Loco chain operates in the southwestern United States, in California.
